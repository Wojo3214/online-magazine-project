Accommodation
  • Airbnb

    • App/website which enables you to find a places in all the world for decent price. Thanks that you can save a lot of money and spend them for more atractives. Because there are many private houses or rooms you can try to negotiate the price. To sum up, there is a huge possibility that you spend your time in beautiful place for low price.   • Couchsurfing

    • It’s a social platform where people from around the world announce that they can host you in their private houses. Its purpose is helping each other and getting new people and their cultures. What’s the best you don’t have to pay for it. However, remember to not treat them as hotel as it isn’t.

Flight search
  • Skyscanner

    • One of the best flight search which makes possible to find a best prices for your flight in one of the fastest ways. Thanks many options which are include in this app you can find them not only flight tickets but also hotels and tour suggestions. However, I recommend find tickets in this app and after that buy once on air carrier’s website. It will be cheap as you’ll not pay for intermediary.
