New skyscraper in Dubai.

Posted on: 31 Oct 2019 By Wojciech Dzwończyk

Dubai is well known for doing everything the tallest in the world. Now there is the same. They build the tallest tower in the world which will be taller than Burj Khalifa.

We don’t know how tall it will be but we can be sure that when it will be finished it will have more than 828 metres (Burj Khalifa). Whole costs of this construction is around $1 billion. What is more, the construction will rotate 360 degree thanks that there will be possibility to see the view of whole city.
