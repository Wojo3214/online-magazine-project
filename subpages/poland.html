Suggested places to see in Poland

Posted on: 31 Oct 2019 By Wojciech Dzwończyk

First what comes to your mind when you think about Poland is Warsaw or pierogi. I guess that one special word as well but not about it. In this article I would like to show you few places which are worth seeing in this beautiful country. Maybe one day you will visit them.

Cracow

Let’s start from Cracow. The second capital of Poland in the past. First place which you must see (yes exactly, MUST see) is Wawel castle. The castle was finished in XVII centuries and it was king’s residency. If you watched the Game of thrones and you're not sick of dragons, you can meet one in this place.
